## How AI Sentiment is Formed

AI platforms synthesize sentiment from:
1. **Your own published content** (what you say about yourself)
2. **Third-party reviews and press coverage** (what others say about you)
3. **Structured data signals** (ratings, review schema)
4. **How you address criticism** (response content, FAQs about issues)
5. **Comparison mentions** (how you're described relative to competitors)
