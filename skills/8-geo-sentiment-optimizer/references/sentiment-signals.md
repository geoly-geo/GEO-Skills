# Sentiment Signals Reference

## Positive Signals

- Clear value proposition in first paragraph
- Specific customer outcomes with numbers
- Social proof (customer count, ratings)
- Awards and recognitions named
- Trust signals (founded date, team size)

## Negative Signals

- Vague "we're the best" claims
- Passive voice throughout
- No customer testimonials
- Generic descriptions
- AI vocabulary patterns

## Fix Priority

1. Replace vague claims with specific proof
2. Add customer outcomes and data
3. Address negatives proactively in FAQ
4. Use active voice
5. Add social proof
